Salad recipe

Ingredients:-

  • cucumber
  • tomato
  • avocado
  • salad
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • cliantro
  • salt
  • black paper

Instruction steps to make this salad are:-

  1. We are going to start by cutting our vegetables
  2. Put all your mixed vegetables in a large bowl
  3. Add 1 slice of avocado and cilantro
  4. In separate bowl, place all the dressing ingredients and whisk until combined
  5. Mix the salad with the dressing
  6. Plate your salad

I like this salad recipe because it's very healthy and delicious.
